Problem

EU launch workflows break when required documents are missing.

APAC sellers can move fast on listings, but EU launch readiness requires evidence and documentation discipline. Missing items appear late and create expensive rework loops.

Main Pain Points

  • Listing teams discover missing documentation near go-live.
  • Review cycles bounce between ops, compliance partners, and engineering.
  • Teams lack one deterministic checklist output to act on quickly.
  • Managers struggle to prove whether compliance workflow is improving.

Cost Of Staying Manual

  • Launch delays and blocked listings.
  • More manual review work per SKU.
  • Higher risk of noisy last-minute fixes.
  • Weak pilot-to-paid conversion proof for new tooling.
