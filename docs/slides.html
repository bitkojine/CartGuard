Definition

What is a "claim"?

A claim is any statement on a product page that can influence buyer trust or legal risk.

  • Examples: "clinically proven", "reduces wrinkles", "clean", "dermatologist tested"
  • If a claim cannot be supported, it can become a compliance issue.
  • CartGuard treats each claim as a checkable object with source + policy rules.

Regulatory Baseline

EU cosmetics claims must be evidence-based.

Cosmetics claims are not free-form marketing text. They are regulated statements that must be supported.
