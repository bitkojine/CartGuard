Problem

Cosmetics marketing risk is expensive.

  • More AI-written product copy every week
  • Manual review is slow and inconsistent
  • Weak or unsupported claims can reach production
  • When issues hit regulators, the cost can be huge

Regulatory Baseline

EU cosmetics claims must be evidence-based.

Cosmetics claims are not free-form marketing text. They are regulated statements that must be supported.

  • Reported L'Oréal case figure: €189.5M (French antitrust history)
  • Reported L'Oréal + peers settlement: ~€10M collective (Turkey)

Competitor Geography

Most AI content competitors are US-first and generic.

  • Jasper, Copy.ai, Writer: broad AI platforms, not EU cosmetics claim gates
  • Salsify, Syndigo: product content platforms, not CI claim enforcement tools
  • Their center of gravity is broad/global workflows, not EU claim nuance

Solution

CartGuard = CI for claims.

  • Typed claim schema
  • Policy-as-code validation
  • Source-linked evidence output
  • Pass/fail gate before publish

How This Reduces Fine Risk

We target the failure points that lead to expensive mistakes.

  • Unsupported claim text gets blocked before it ships.
  • High-risk claim categories can require stronger source evidence.
  • Every decision is logged with policy rule IDs and source links.
  • Compliance can review evidence before launch, not after escalation.
  • Teams get repeatable checks instead of ad-hoc manual judgment.
