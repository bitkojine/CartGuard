Strategy

One wedge: APAC mid-sized ecommerce teams entering Europe.

Start with developers in CI. Expand to compliance and operations after proof.

Who We Target First

Region: APAC -> EU Company size: mid-sized teams Entry buyer: Head of Engineering Co-buyer: Compliance/Legal lead

Why This Segment First

  • They move fast and feel launch delays immediately.
  • They have enough volume to need automation, but are still agile enough to adopt quickly.
  • Their pain is concrete: missing documents discovered too late.
  • A developer-first workflow can be deployed without a full platform migration.

Developer-First Motion

Engineers own release pipelines and can add CartGuard early in the flow. Once missing-document reports are trusted, compliance and operations teams adopt the same outputs. This gives us fast entry and broader expansion inside accounts.

Pilot Success Metrics

  • Fewer missing documents at release time.
  • Faster internal review cycle from draft to publish.
  • Cleaner, repeatable evidence reports per product and market.
  • Clear owner of budget for rollout after pilot.

Focus Discipline

We are intentionally not expanding the website message to every vertical right now. This wedge stays APAC-to-EU migration until pilots prove repeatable demand.
