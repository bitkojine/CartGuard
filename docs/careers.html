Careers

Who we need and what each role does every day.

This page is the operating manual for hiring and ownership. We hire for clear outcomes, not vague responsibilities.

Founder Reality (Day 2)

Founders will wear many hats right now, and that is expected. These role definitions are still useful because they organize the work, clarify ownership, and show what responsibilities must be covered as we grow.

How We Hire

  • Each role must map to pilot conversion outcomes.
  • Every person owns weekly deliverables that are visible in CRM.
  • No role is "support only"; every role must push one core metric.
