Roadmap

Day 2 startup roadmap: improve every day, not all at once.

This page turns stress into execution. We track what must be true each week to prove CartGuard can become a real business.

Current stage: early validation. We are building proof, not pretending certainty.

Where We Are Now

  • Company age: day 2.
  • Positioning selected: APAC sellers entering EU marketplaces.
  • Biggest gap: not enough pilot outcome evidence yet.

Daily And Weekly Cadence

  • Daily: one customer-facing action, one product improvement, one evidence update.
  • Twice weekly: pilot/customer review and decision log.
  • Weekly: update weaknesses register and kill one weak assumption.
  • Weekly: publish scoreboard update on pilot metrics.

Roadmap Scoreboard (Update Weekly)

  • Qualified pipeline accounts: target 30-50.
  • Active pilots: target 3.
  • Pilot-to-paid conversion: target 30%+.
  • Median review-time reduction in pilot accounts: target 20%+.

Anti-Stress Operating Rules

  • No panic pivots without evidence.
  • No new feature work unless linked to a pilot metric.
  • No big claims without source-backed proof.
  • Every week must end with one clear business learning.
