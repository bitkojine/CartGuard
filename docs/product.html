Product Developers Strategy

Product

Spec-first validation for AI-generated cosmetics claims.

No hidden rules. Claims, policies, and outcomes are explicit and auditable.

The Problem

  • AI-generated claims are often unverifiable.
  • Manual review does not scale with listing volume.
  • Compliance checks happen too late in release cycles.

The CartGuard Approach

  • Model claims as typed, source-linked objects.
  • Enforce explicit policy rules, not implicit heuristics.
  • Gate listings in CI using machine-readable results.
