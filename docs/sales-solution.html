Solution

Structured document-gap checks built into your launch workflow.

CartGuard scans listing payloads against policy rulepacks and flags missing required evidence before publish steps.

CartGuard does not decide what is legal. It recommends what evidence appears missing.

How It Works

Step What Happens
1. IngestRead product content and associated document metadata.
2. CheckRun policy checks for required evidence presence.
3. OutputReturn missing-item list, severity, and recommended next actions.
4. TrackMeasure missing-doc rate and review-time trend during pilot.

What Teams Get

  • Clear list of missing documents before launch handoff.
  • Lower manual rework between teams.
  • Deterministic, repeatable output for pilot reviews.
  • Metrics needed for convert-or-stop decisions.
