Competition

Where we win and lose in APAC to EU market entry.

Positioning against real alternatives, not fantasy competitors.

Executive Summary

  • Generic AI writing tools create content but do not manage missing-document workflows for EU entry.
  • PIM/PXM suites are strong systems of record, but documentation checks are not their core developer workflow.
  • Manual legal review is trusted, but slow and hard to scale across markets.
  • In-house scripts are a serious competitor because they feel cheaper at first.
  • Our wedge: developer-first CI checks that flag missing evidence before publish.

Why We Could Lose

  • Buyers may trust existing manual process more than a new tool.
  • In-house scripts can be "good enough" for a while.
  • Platform vendors can absorb parts of our workflow over time.
  • If onboarding is slow, teams will skip us.
  • If outputs are noisy, compliance will not trust recommendations.

Why We Can Win

  • Clear wedge: missing-document checks for APAC-to-EU launch flows.
  • Developer-first integration in CI is fast to adopt.
  • Outputs are machine-readable and review-friendly.
  • No need for full CMS/PIM migration to start.
  • Strong fit for teams with repeated cross-border launches.

Best Positioning (Now)

Pitch line: "CartGuard flags missing evidence before APAC sellers publish into EU markets."

Wedge message: "Stop finding missing documents at the last minute. Catch them in CI before launch review."

  • Do not claim legal determinations. Claim missing-evidence recommendations.
  • Stay focused on APAC mid-sized teams entering Europe.
  • Win with speed: easy setup, clear reports, low false noise.
