Pricing

Pricing model and ranges (early-stage).

These are working ranges for pilots and early customers, not final long-term pricing.

Recommended Pricing Model Now

Use a subscription model first. Avoid revenue-share in the early stage. Revenue-share adds complexity and slows deals.

Primary: monthly subscription Optional: usage tier by claim volume Avoid now: % of revenue model

Suggested Price Ranges (To Test)

Plan Target Monthly Price What Is Included
Pilot First design partners €1,000 - €3,000 Core claim checks, 1-2 integrations, weekly support
Growth Mid-size ecommerce teams €3,000 - €8,000 Higher volume, policy packs, audit artifacts, SLA-lite
Enterprise Multi-market orgs €10,000+ Advanced controls, custom integrations, security review, SLA

Alternative Models (Maybe Later)

  • Usage-based: charge per validated claim or per policy run.
  • Hybrid: base subscription + usage overage.
  • Revenue-share: not recommended right now; hard to measure and negotiate.

What We Still Need To Validate

  • Who owns budget first: Engineering, Compliance, or Operations.
  • Price sensitivity by team size and SKU volume.
  • Which value metric buyers trust most: incidents avoided vs time saved.
  • Whether annual prepay discounts increase conversion.

Short Pitch Answer On Pricing

"We start with subscription pricing. Pilot customers pay monthly while we prove risk reduction and faster reviews. Then we scale to tiered plans by claim volume and integration depth."
