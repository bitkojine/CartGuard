Why CartGuard View Architecture

TypeScript SDK • Policy Engine • Build-Time Gate

The Problem

AI-generated cosmetics listings are scaling, but claim quality is inconsistent and legal exposure is rising across EU markets.

  • Anti-greenwashing enforcement pressure
  • Cross-border policy fragmentation
  • Unverifiable AI claims in production catalogs

The CartGuard Approach

Encode trust as code. Claims are typed objects with sources. Policies are explicit. Validation is deterministic and CI-native.

  • Spec-first domain model
  • Policy-driven engine (no hidden constants)
  • Machine-readable pass/fail outputs
