The Problem We Solve

APAC teams can generate product content quickly, but EU market entry requires proof and documentation that is often missing at publish time. CartGuard flags those missing items early so launches do not get delayed at final review.

Why APAC to EU Is Our First Wedge

The pain is concentrated here: fast launch velocity from APAC teams and strict documentation expectations in Europe. This creates an immediate workflow gap we can solve with developer tools.

High cross-border listing volume Country-by-country evidence expectations Manual review bottlenecks Delayed launches from missing documents

Real Pain Teams Feel Today

  • "We can create content fast, but EU review still blocks launch."
  • "We cannot quickly see which evidence is missing by product and country."
  • "Engineering and legal work from different checklists and tools."
  • "Missing docs are found too late, after launch plans are set."

What We Know And What We Still Need To Prove

We know the technical part works: teams can run checks in CI and flag missing evidence. What we still need to prove is business demand: how many teams will pay now, how much this reduces launch delays, and which buyer owns budget first.
