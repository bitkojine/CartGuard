The Problem We Solve

Many product pages now use AI text. Some claims can be wrong, unsupported, or risky. If those claims go live, companies face customer trust issues and legal trouble. CartGuard checks claims before publish so bad claims are caught early.

Why We Picked EU Cosmetics First

Cosmetics teams have high claim pressure and high risk at the same time. They publish fast, they use lots of claim-heavy language, and mistakes are expensive.

Many claims per product page Cross-country listing complexity High trust sensitivity with consumers Legal and brand risk when claims are wrong

Real Pain Teams Feel Today

  • \"We are shipping content fast but review is still manual.\"
  • \"We do not have one clear place to check if claims are safe.\"
  • \"Legal gets involved too late, right before launch.\"
  • \"We cannot easily prove where each claim came from.\"
  • \"Cross-border rules make content approval slow and messy.\"

What We Know And What We Still Need To Prove

We know the technical part works: teams can run checks in CI and block risky claims. What we still need to prove is business demand: how many teams will pay now, how much this reduces real incidents, and which buyer owns budget first.
