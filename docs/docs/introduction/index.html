Introduction

CartGuard helps listing teams catch missing EU-required documents before Amazon.de submission, so launches avoid review delays and rework loops.

Recommendation-only checks. CartGuard does not provide legal advice or legal determinations.

Open Domain Concepts Open Ops Manual

What CartGuard Does

  • Reads listing and product data before publish/submission steps.
  • Flags missing required documents based on configured check rules.
  • Shows blockers by SKU/listing with clear next actions for operators.
  • Tracks workflow metrics: missing-document rate, review time, and rework count.

What CartGuard Does Not Do

  • Does not issue legal determinations or legal opinions.
  • Does not replace legal counsel or regulatory authorities.
  • Does not auto-approve listings without customer workflow review.
