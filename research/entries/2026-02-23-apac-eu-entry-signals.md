## Key Facts
- EU low-value consignments (<= EUR150): 4.6B in 2024.
- Share from China: 91% in 2024.
- Estimated 2025 level: 5.8B.
- Enforcement intensity in EU marketplaces is active and increasing.

## Notes
- No single official dataset provides a direct count of "Asian companies migrating to Europe".
